### Purpose

This page provides contact information and links to various social media platforms for the brand.

### Inputs

None.

### Outputs

A visually appealing contact page with a plasma background, brand logo, and links to LINE, Website, and Instagram.

### Dependencies

- `react` for UI components and hooks.
- `next/link` for internal navigation (though not directly used in provided links).
- `next/image` for optimized image display.

### Usage

Users can access this page to find ways to contact or follow the brand.
